Exploring User Needs Through Comprehensive Research

I conducted in-depth interviews, focus group discussions, and field studies to uncover valuable user insights for offering and creating leasing contracts. These methods provided a deep understanding of customer pain points and opportunities, forming the foundation for a user-focused solution.

Collaborative Design Thinking to Solve Complex Problems

Working with the team, I applied design thinking to explore and refine problems through divergent and convergent approaches. This process heavily relied on my expertise in facilitating workshops, enabling effective collaboration and idea generation. I then synthesized the research into a clear, actionable format, making it easy for stakeholders to grasp key insights.

Delivering Results: A Mobile Leasing App Driving Revenue

The result was a tablet-based leasing app designed for mobility, allowing users to offer and create leasing contracts anytime, anywhere. This innovative solution generated significant impact, driving revenue of 6 million Baht within the first month of launch.
