Uncovering Critical Task Management Challenges

UX research revealed key issues: the need for tools to create, track, and assign recurring tasks; challenges in balancing task assignments for drone pilots; difficulty in locating created tasks; and discrepancies between the system's recorded completion times and the actual times reported by drone pilots.

Transforming Insights into Action: Crafting Solutions Through Collaboration

Afterward, I created service blueprints and user journey maps to address user pain points, collaborating closely with the Product Owner and development team to craft effective solutions.

From Concept to Creation, Designing User-Centric Solutions through Testing

Finally, I created paper wireframes followed by concept testing. After validating the designs with users, I developed high-fidelity wireframes and conducted usability tests. The result is a project management website and LINE OA that effectively addresses user needs.
